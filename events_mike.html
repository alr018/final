DJ Mike Czech

Air Conditioned Lounge

December 13th, 2014

Saturday 12am

$5

He’s a national radio DJ with a show syndicated in 60+ cities and counting…and he’s got nearly 15 years experience DJ'ing in clubs across San Diego and America. He's been the fixture on Saturdays at the AIR CONDITIONED Lounge for 7 years and keeps the packed house movin' and groovin' and comin' back for more week in and week out.

He’s a math geek. He grows his own veggies. He’s a father and a husband.

Mike Czech is balanced in his personal life--and that balance transcends into his DJ sets both in the club and onto his hit radio rock and hip hop show, Skratch and Sniff.

As the epitome of a well-rounded DJ, Mike Czech has truly done it all. And many local San Diego DJ’s acknowledge the fact that Mike was a part of a core group who helped to blaze a trail for them.

Looking ahead Mike Czech will continue to branch out and touch other cities with his innovative mixes and his focus on getting the crowd MOVING! All the while staying in touch with the people who rock out to his music at the AIR CONDITIONED Lounge!

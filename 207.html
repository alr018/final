207 Nightclub

About

207 Fifth Avenue, San Diego, CA 92101 (at the Hardrock Hotel) | Directions
619-764-6924 | Website

207, located on the ground floor of the Hard Rock Hotel San Diego, is a "laid-back den of glamour featuring a dramatic bar with black leather accents and over-sized crystal chandeliers." Unwind over cocktails or watch a game on the largest TV screen in San Diego. Friday through Sunday nights, 207 guests are treated to music spun by DJs. Happy hour specials Monday through Friday from 3pm to 7pm.
